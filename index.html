BreakCol

BreakCol is a fun match-three game, where you have to arrange three same colored blocks in a row or column from the falling blocks to clear the blocks. If the block falls outside the yellow container then the Game is Over.

How to play:

Tap on the left side of the board to move the Yellow container to the left side.

Tap on the right side to move the container to the right side.

You can use a swipe left or right to move in the corresponding direction.

You can swipe and hold to move the container faster.

Make some high scores and share them with your friends.
