6561 is a fun Number puzzle game

How to play:

Swipe to move the tiles. When three tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one. Reach the 6561 tiles and keep playing to unlock higher-value number tiles!

The sequence will be 1,3,9,27,81,243,729,2187,6561.
